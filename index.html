Scoring Methodology

Candidate generation: 132 district HQs + 76 highway corridor points + 9 emerging zones = 217 candidates.
Auto-scoring: Each pillar computed from actual data — vehicle registrations × fuel consumption → demand; outlets per lakh → competition (inverted); per-capita GDP → income; EV registration share → EV readiness. All percentile-ranked.
Network optimizer: Greedy selection with spacing, budget, and diversity constraints.
